Test the Motors


1. Open LaserGRBL or UGS
2. Connect to Arduino UNO
4. Write in the mini box (command box)
G0 X10   [Observation: X-axis Stepper motor moves]
G0 Y10  [Observation: Y-axis Stepper motor moves]
m3 s010  [Observation: Servo move 10 degree]
m3 s090  [Observation: Servo move 10 degree]

Note:
1. All the Motors will move smoothly.
2. Also you can test the Stepper motor by buttons (left-bottom)
