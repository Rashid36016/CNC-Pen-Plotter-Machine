Wiring


Stepper Motor -> CNC Shield
X Motor:
Connect to X-axis motor port
Y Motor:
Connect to Y-axis motor port


Stepper Drivers:
Insert A4988/DRV8825 correctly
IMPORTANT:
Do NOT reverse driver direction


Servo -> CNC Shield
Signal -> D11
VCC -> 5V
GND -> GND


Power Supply:
12V SMPS -> CNC Shield power input


USB:
PC -> Arduino UNO
